Best Beaches in Koh Phangan

From lively party beaches to quiet tropical coves, Koh Phangan has a beach for every style of traveler. This guide helps you choose where to swim, relax and stay.

📖 Read Main Guide 💰 Compare Tours

Top Beach Areas

Choose your beach based on the vibe you want: nightlife, hidden scenery or a more upscale stay.

Haad Rin

The most famous beach on the island and the center of Full Moon Party energy. Best for nightlife and social travelers.

Bottle Beach

A quieter and more scenic choice for travelers looking for a tropical escape away from the busiest areas.

Thong Nai Pan

One of the best areas for a more polished beach holiday, with beautiful views and a calmer atmosphere.

Beach planning tips

  • For nightlife: stay near Haad Rin.
  • For quiet scenery: choose a more secluded beach area.
  • For a balanced trip: split your stay between a lively area and a peaceful one.
