Koh Phangan Nightlife Guide

Koh Phangan is famous for its beach nightlife, monthly events and fun traveler atmosphere. This page covers the island’s best-known party experiences and how to plan them smartly.

🌕 Full Moon Guide 🎟️ Book Party Transport

Best Nightlife Experiences

Full Moon Party

The island’s biggest nightlife event and one of the most famous beach parties in Thailand.

Beach Bars

For a more casual evening, beach bars are a great choice for sunset drinks, music and a relaxed crowd.

Party Week Atmosphere

The days around Full Moon are often busy across the island, so booking transport and accommodation early helps a lot.

Nightlife tips

  • Book Full Moon transport and tours early.
  • Stay near Haad Rin if nightlife is your main priority.
  • Keep valuables secure and use common sense after late nights.
