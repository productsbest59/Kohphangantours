About Koh Phangan

Koh Phangan is much more than just a party island. It offers relaxed beach towns, beautiful viewpoints, snorkeling spots, jungle roads and some of the best island sunsets in Thailand.

Why travelers love Koh Phangan

Koh Phangan is one of the most popular islands in the Gulf of Thailand. It is famous for the Full Moon Party at Haad Rin, but it also has peaceful beaches, hidden coves, yoga retreats, boat trips and snorkeling tours. Travelers often combine nightlife with relaxing beach days and island-hopping experiences.

  • Best known for the Full Moon Party and beach nightlife
  • Great for snorkeling, boat trips and sunset cruises
  • Offers both party areas and quiet relaxing beaches
  • Easy to combine with Koh Samui and Koh Tao

Best Beaches in Koh Phangan

These are some of the most popular beaches on the island, depending on whether you want nightlife, swimming, quiet views or a more upscale atmosphere.

Haad Rin

The most famous beach on the island and home of the Full Moon Party. Best for nightlife, events and travelers who want action.

Bottle Beach

A more secluded beach with a laid-back atmosphere. Great for people looking for beautiful scenery and a quieter escape.

Thong Nai Pan

One of the best areas for a more comfortable stay. Good for couples, families and travelers who want a calmer vibe.

Full Moon Party Guide

The Full Moon Party is the island’s most famous event. If it is your first time, it helps to know where it happens, how to plan and what to book in advance.

What to expect

Large crowds, beach bars, music stages and a high-energy atmosphere. It is one of the best-known beach parties in the world.

Best tip

Book transport and tours early around Full Moon dates. Demand is usually much higher during party week.

Full Moon Party tips

  • Stay close to Haad Rin if nightlife is your priority
  • Book transport or tours early on Full Moon dates
  • Bring cash and keep valuables secure
  • Wear comfortable shoes and stay hydrated

Where To Stay in Koh Phangan

Choose your base depending on what kind of trip you want: party, relaxation, beach time or a mix of everything.

Best areas by travel style

  • Haad Rin: best for party travelers and Full Moon dates
  • Thong Nai Pan: better for a calmer and more comfortable stay
  • Central areas: useful if you want easier access around the island

Many travelers stay near Haad Rin during party dates and then move to a quieter beach area for the rest of the trip.

How To Get to Koh Phangan

Most travelers reach Koh Phangan through Koh Samui or Surat Thani and then continue by ferry.

Via Koh Samui

A common option for travelers who want the fastest and easiest island connection.

Via Surat Thani

Often a more budget-friendly route for travelers combining bus, train or ferry connections.

Useful Travel Tips

Best time to visit: Koh Phangan is popular year-round, but weather and crowd levels vary by season. Party dates usually mean higher demand and more bookings.

What to book first: Full Moon transport, popular tours and accommodation close to party dates.

Good strategy: Spend a few nights in a lively area and then move to a quieter beach for the rest of your stay.
