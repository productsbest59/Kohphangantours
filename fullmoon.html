Full Moon Party Guide

The Full Moon Party is the most famous event in Koh Phangan. Here is what to expect, how to plan, and where to book transport and experiences in advance.

🎟️ Book Full Moon Tour 🚤 Compare on Viator

What to Expect

Beach Party Energy

Large crowds, music, lights and a high-energy beach atmosphere make this the island’s signature nightlife experience.

Busy Dates

Transport, hotels and tours become much busier around Full Moon dates, so advance booking is strongly recommended.

Plan Your Night

Think about where you stay, how you get back, and how much you want to carry with you on the beach.

Best Full Moon tips

  • Book transport in advance for the smoothest experience.
  • Bring only what you need.
  • Stay hydrated and wear comfortable shoes.
  • If it is your first time, booking an organized option is often easier.
